About

Learn about the CI Needs project, the team behind it, and the mission to support every Dolphin on campus.

Our Mission

CI Needs is a peer-to-peer student resource network built for the Cal State Channel Islands community. Our mission is to connect students to resources that can help them continue with their education — whether that means finding a meal, a textbook, a laptop, or just knowing that someone in the Dolphin community is ready to help. We believe that no student should have to pause or abandon their academic journey because of a basic need that the community around them could meet. CI Needs makes it simple to ask for help, offer support, and keep each other moving forward.

Meet the Team

CI Needs was designed and built by a team of Master's students in the Computer Science program at California State University Channel Islands.
